How to Read This Dashboard

Z-Score

Std devs from 12mo mean. >+1.5=WATCH, >+2.0=ELEVATED, >+3.0=CRITICAL.

R-Score (0-100)

Composite risk: Z-anomaly(20)+severity(20)+growth gap(20)+slope(20)+IB rate(20). >50=investigate, >70=act.

Rate/$M Revenue

Reports per $M revenue. Rising rate = quality deteriorating faster than growth.

Rate/10K Users

Reports per 10K installed base users. Most precise normalization.

6mo Trend

Linear regression slope. Positive = accelerating. The momentum signal.

Batch Detection

Orange = manufacturer dumped old reports at once. NOT a real surge.

Correlation

Spearman rho vs stock returns. * = p<0.05. Negative = predictive.

Smoothed View

V3.2: Auto-selects best SMA/EWMA to align date_received with date_of_event.
