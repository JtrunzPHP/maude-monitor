How to Read This Dashboard

R-Score (0-100)

Composite risk score. 0=safe, 100=critical. Weights: Z-score (20), trend (20), deaths (20), injuries (20), rate (20). R≥50=investigate, R≥70=act.

Z-Score

Standard deviations from mean. Z≥2=anomalous (95th percentile). Z≥3=extreme. Negative=below average reporting.

Rate/$M Revenue

Reports per $M revenue. Normalizes for business size. RISING rate=quality deteriorating faster than revenue growth.

Rate/10K Users

Reports per 10K installed base. Most precise normalization. Sources: earnings calls, 10-K filings.

6mo Trend (Slope)

Linear regression slope of last 6 months. Positive=accelerating reports. +50 means ~50 more reports/month.

Batch Detection

Orange bars on charts. Original: received>3x event count. NEW: recall-aware detection flags known recall batch windows (e.g. DXCM 700K receiver recall). Prevents false signals.

Multi-Signal Correlation

Tests 6 MAUDE signals (raw, delta, z-score, rate/$M, rate/10K, acceleration) against stock returns at 0-6mo lags. Confidence 0-100. Rate-normalized signals strip installed base growth noise.

Case Study Backtest

Shows specific trade signals: date, entry price, exit price, P&L. Grade (STRONG/MODERATE/WEAK) based on hit rate. Batch/recall months auto-excluded.
